Contact Snap

Snap a photo of a business card. Save the contact instantly.

macOS · Menu bar app · Powered by AI

How it works

Three steps from card to contact — no typing required.

📸

Snap or import

Click the Contact Snap icon in your menu bar, then take a photo with your iPhone's camera or drop in an image of any business card.

✨

AI reads the card

Gemini AI extracts the name, title, company, email, phone, address, and website — accurately, in seconds.

📇

Saved to Apple Contacts

A complete contact record appears in your Mac's address book. Review the details before saving, then it syncs to all your Apple devices.

Get it

A free download for Macs running macOS Ventura or later.

💻

Contact Snap for Mac

Works on Apple Silicon and Intel Macs. Requires macOS Ventura (13) or later. The app lives in your menu bar — always one click away.
